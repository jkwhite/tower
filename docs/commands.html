List of Important Commands

These basic commands are enough to get started in the Tower. The complete command set as described in the manual is much more interesting.

Movement - h,j,k,l or arrow keys

Pick something up - , (comma)

Drop something - d

Display inventory - i

Wield a weapon - w

Wear a piece of armor - W

Eat something - e

Drink something - q

Read a scroll - r

Zap a wand - z

Use a tool - a

Throw something - t

Take off a piece of armor - T

Ascend a staircase - <

Descend a staircase - > (period)

Open a door - o

Impolitely kick down a door - Ctrl+d

Do nothing - . (period)
